Kraken Cryptocurrency Exchange Dashboard Concept

Overview

Kraken is a cryptocurrency exchange focused on providing its users with a secure and reliable platform for buying and selling cryptocurrencies. Cryptocurrency is a new space, but it brings with it a host of old problems familiar to financial technology. Though traders no longer gather in crowds on trading floors, much of the same clutter and disarray can still be found on computer screens.
